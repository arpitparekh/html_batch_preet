Political campaigns often target specific demographics to sway public opinion. This strategy is evident in both domestic and international elections. Effective campaigns utilize data analytics to reach voters.

On the other hand, negative campaigning has become a common tactic, often overshadowing policy discussions with personal attacks and sensationalism. This can lead to a misinformed electorate.

The Role of Media in Politics

The media plays a crucial role in shaping political narratives. News outlets and journalists have the power to inform the public, hold politicians accountable, and foster democratic discourse. However, the rise of fake news and biased reporting poses significant challenges.

Conclusion
